Overcoming Challenges in AI-Driven QA

While the future looks promising, it’s essential to address challenges associated with widespread AI adoption in QA:

- **Over-dependence on Automation:** Human expertise is critical for unforeseen, nuanced issues despite AI capabilities.
- **AI Bias:** Ensuring impartial algorithm training data is key to fair and comprehensive QA practices.
- **Data Privacy:** Robust privacy policies must safeguard sensitive information during AI-testing phases.
- **Reskilling the Workforce:** Empowering QA professionals to adapt and manage AI-centric workflows.
- **Global Regulations:** The need for ethical protocols and transparency in AI’s growth.
