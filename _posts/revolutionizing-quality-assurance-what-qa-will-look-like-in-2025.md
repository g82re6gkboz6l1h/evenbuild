Over the years, QA has shifted from manual testing to automated testing, streamlining workflows and embracing Agile and DevOps methodologies to catch issues early in the software development lifecycle (SDLC). However, the need for efficiency continues to grow as digital demands rise. By leveraging machine learning and data analytics, the future of QA will emphasize delivering robust user experiences via personalized and predictive testing processes.
