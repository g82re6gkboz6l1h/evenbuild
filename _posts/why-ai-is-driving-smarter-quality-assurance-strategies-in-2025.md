## The Role of AI in 2025 QA Processes

AI is increasingly becoming indispensable in QA for its ability to handle complex and large-scale testing scenarios. Here’s how it’s making an impact:

2. **Advanced Accuracy:** AI detects vulnerabilities and anomalies that manual testing may miss, ensuring higher-quality software delivery.
3. **Scalability:** AI tests complex software, from IoT devices to enterprise solutions, without increasing manpower requirements.
4. **Smart Bug Detection:** Machine learning enables AI to pinpoint problematic code patterns and predict potential deficiencies before release.
