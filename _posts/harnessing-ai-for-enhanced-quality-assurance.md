## Key Benefits of AI in Defect Detection

### 1. Proactive Error Identification
AI algorithms sift through data, identifying subtle issues before they become major bugs.

### 2. Enhanced Pattern Recognition
AI tools detect recurring code anomalies, reducing repeat occurrences of core defects.

### 3. Real-Time Monitoring
Continuous testing offers immediate feedback, catching glitches and improving performance.

### 4. Reduced False Positives and Negatives
AI systems refine accuracy over time, minimizing error misclassification during testing.
