These capabilities allow QA teams to detect defects before they escalate into larger-scale problems.

Modern tools, including Zof AI, exemplify how ML technologies are streamlining QA operations. By analyzing historical data and real-time behaviors, such systems provide actionable insights, identify trends, and reduce inefficiencies.
