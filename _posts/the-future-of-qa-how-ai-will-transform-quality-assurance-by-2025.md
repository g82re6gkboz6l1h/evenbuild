AI is ushering innovative changes across industries, and QA is no exception. Here’s how AI is reshaping the field:

- **Defect Prediction**: Algorithms pinpoint vulnerable sections of code, reducing defects in production.
- **Self-Healing Automation**: AI frameworks autonomously repair broken or outdated tests.
- **Efficient Regression Testing**: Target specific areas for updates without redundant efforts.
