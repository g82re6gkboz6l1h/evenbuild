## Transforming QA Practices

### From QA to Quality Engineering (QE)
QA teams will transform into cross-functional Quality Engineering units that merge testing, development, and business objectives, emphasizing user experience and faster delivery cycles.

### Automation and Continuous Testing
The rise of advanced test automation and continuous testing will reduce bottlenecks. Teams will adopt intelligent automation, and continuous testing in DevSecOps will enhance security and data protection.

### Global Collaboration Tools
Remote and hybrid work models will require QA tools that support seamless global team collaboration.

## AI and ML Revolution in QA
By 2025, AI and ML will redefine software testing:

- **Automated Defect Detection**: AI algorithms will quickly identify bugs within codebases.
- **Test Case Optimization**: Machine learning improves test case prioritization.
- **Predictive Analytics**: AI tools predict risks and assist in better resource use.
- **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**: Dynamic test cases based on natural language inputs.
- **Self-Healing Test Scripts**: ML-powered tools automatically adjust scripts to match code changes.
