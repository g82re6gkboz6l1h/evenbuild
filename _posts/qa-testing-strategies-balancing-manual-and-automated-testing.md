## Understanding Manual vs. Automated Testing

Achieve better QA outcomes by understanding the differences between manual and automated testing. Manual testing excels at user-centric tasks, exploratory scenarios, and edge case detection. On the other hand, automated testing delivers speed, accuracy, and efficiency for repetitive tasks. Combining the strengths of both via hybrid strategies ensures comprehensive QA coverage.
