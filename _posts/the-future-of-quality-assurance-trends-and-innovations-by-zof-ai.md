## The Evolution of QA: From Manual Testing to AI-Driven Processes

**A Historical Look at QA Innovation**
Quality assurance originated in the 1950s as manual testing, evolving into automated workflows with tools like Selenium by the mid-90s. Fast forward, DevOps and CI/CD have made QA more integrated into development pipelines, promoting a shift-left mentality. Despite advancements, growing software complexity requires intelligent tools, positioning **Zof AI** as a game-changer in modern QA.
