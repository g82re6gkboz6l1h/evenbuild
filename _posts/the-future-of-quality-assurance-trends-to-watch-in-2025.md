## Introduction: A New Era for QA

In the past, QA primarily focused on identifying and resolving issues post-development. Now, the focus is shifting toward proactive approaches, ensuring faultless software from the get-go. Modern QA methods are leveraging AI, automation, and data analytics, creating smarter, faster, and highly efficient frameworks. The integration of these technologies is reshaping the QA landscape to meet the demands of increasingly complex software ecosystems and heightened customer expectations.
