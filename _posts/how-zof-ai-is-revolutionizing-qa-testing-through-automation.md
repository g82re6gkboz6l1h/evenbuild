### 2. Inconsistencies and Oversights

### 3. Costly Workflow
Traditional QA incurs high costs due to personnel, infrastructure, and inefficient resource allocation.

### 4. Difficulty Scaling
When projects grow or updates need frequent releases, traditional methods often fail to match speed and complexity requirements.
