## Embracing Modern QA Practices

### Key Trends Transforming QA
- **Shift-Left Testing:** Identify defects early in the lifecycle to avoid post-production costs.
- **DevOps & Continuous Integration (CI/CD):** Seamless automation between development and QA.
- **User-Centric Testing:** Focus beyond code on accessibility, security, and performance.
- **Rise of Test Automation Tools:** Frameworks like Selenium and Appium become essential.

### Challenges Addressed by Predictive AI Technology

Despite streamlined processes, QA teams struggle with scalability, error prevention, and proactive bug anticipation—making AI pivotal for future testing.
