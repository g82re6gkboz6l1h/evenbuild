## Evolution of Quality Assurance Practices
QA has evolved from manual testing to automation and now to AI-driven workflows. From time-consuming manual scripts to dynamic AI-powered systems, this shift solves challenges posed by complex applications, multi-platform ecosystems, and fast-paced development. Explore the origins of QA to understand today’s AI-powered shifts.

### Key Insights in AI-Powered QA
1. **Smarter Test Case Design**: AI autonomously generates test cases based on real-world behaviors.
2. **Predictive Analysis**: Predict future issues using past user behaviors and bug reports.
3. **Intelligent Test Execution**: AI dynamically modifies testing strategies.
4. **Visual Testing**: Recognize visual UI anomalies effortlessly.
5. **Self-Healing Systems**: AI auto-corrects issues in test scripts for continuous optimization.
6. **Agile Continuous Testing**: AI integrates seamlessly into CI/CD pipelines, enabling faster workflows.
