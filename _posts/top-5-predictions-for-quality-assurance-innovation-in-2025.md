5. Emerging Tech Trends Shaping QA's Future

Breakthrough technologies beyond AI and DevOps will heavily influence QA by 2025. Here are key areas poised for transformative impact:

#### Blockchain in QA
Blockchain will revolutionize security and audit trails during testing, ensuring data integrity and reducing fraud risks. Immutable records will enhance trust in QA processes.

#### Edge Computing for Decentralized Testing
Edge computing will demand quicker localized testing strategies to validate applications operating closer to their users, ensuring efficiency across decentralized networks.

#### IoT Testing
With billions of connected devices expected by 2025, IoT testing will revolve around interoperability, security testing, and scalability within hyperconnected infrastructures.

#### AR/VR Testing
Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) testing will grow, focusing on immersion sensitivity, performance consistency, and responsiveness in simulated environments.
