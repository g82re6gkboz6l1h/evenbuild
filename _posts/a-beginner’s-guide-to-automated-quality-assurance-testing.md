### Manual vs Automated QA Testing: A Comparison

Understanding the differences between **manual testing** and **automated testing** is key to choosing the right approach.

#### Manual Testing: Human Expertise
- **Advantages**: Flexibility for usability testing and identifying unique user behavior.
- **Disadvantages**: Slower, prone to error, and challenging for repetitive tasks or complex scenarios.

#### Automated Testing: Speed and Precision
- **Advantages**: Faster execution, consistent test coverage, and scalable solutions for modern software.
- **Disadvantages**: Initial setup investment and maintenance needs.

Automated testing overcomes many challenges of manual testing and is ideal for teams aiming for efficiency and adaptability.
