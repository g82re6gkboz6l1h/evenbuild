Many organizations face significant obstacles when implementing QA automation, including:

### 1. **Complexity of Test Scripts**
Creating and maintaining scripts for diverse environments and platforms demands high technical expertise, delaying testing cycles.

Frequent software updates require constant test case maintenance, adding undue stress and cost to QA teams.

### 3. **Scalability Issues**
As applications grow in size and complexity, traditional testing tools struggle to keep up, leading to reduced efficiency.

### 4. **False Positives and Negatives**
Inaccurate results waste time and resources, undermining confidence in automation.

### 5. **Lack of CI/CD Integration**
Poor compatibility with modern DevOps pipelines creates bottlenecks.
