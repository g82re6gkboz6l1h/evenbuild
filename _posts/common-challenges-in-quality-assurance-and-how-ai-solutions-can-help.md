Limited resources, evolving requirements, insufficient test coverage, and scalability challenges often plague traditional QA workflows.

### Common QA Challenges

- **Limited Resources:** Tight timelines require prioritizing critical areas, leaving gaps in test coverage.
- **Evolving Requirements:** Agile methodologies demand frequent test updates to align with shifting goals.
- **Manual Testing Bottlenecks:** Overusing manual methods can lead to errors and inefficiencies.
- **Difficulty in Scaling:** Increased complexity of software strains QA resources and timelines.
- **Error Diagnosis:** Troubleshooting and identifying root causes of bugs require labor-intensive efforts.
