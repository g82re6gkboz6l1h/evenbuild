3. Why Shift-Left Testing Matters

Shift-left testing involves identifying and resolving bugs earlier in the software development lifecycle. This proactive strategy reduces costs, shortens release cycles, and ensures a smoother deployment process.

### **Benefits of Shift-Left Testing**
1. Reduces late-stage defects, saving time and resources.
2. Enhances product quality by addressing issues early.
3. Minimizes delays and risks in production.

### **Zof AI’s Role in Shift-Left Testing**
Zof AI enables teams to implement shift-left practices effortlessly by allowing early test designs and leveraging predictive analytics. This ensures that potential defects are identified and rectified before they escalate into bigger challenges.
