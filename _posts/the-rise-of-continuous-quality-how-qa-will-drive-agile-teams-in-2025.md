## What is Continuous Quality?

Continuous quality represents a monumental shift in QA thinking. Unlike traditional systems where testing happens at the end of development, continuous quality integrates QA across **every stage of the Agile process**. 

### Key Components of Continuous Quality:
1. **Shift-left testing**: Spot errors early by merging QA with development.
2. **Automation**: Automate repetitive testing to increase accuracy.
3. **Real-time monitoring and feedback**: Use live data to refine and perfect software improvements.

These principles empower Agile teams to release superior software more frequently while maintaining the utmost quality.
