## 2. Elevating Scalability with Cloud-Based Testing

Cloud-based testing is setting a new benchmark for scalability and global accessibility. Testing environments dynamically expand or contract based on real-time demands, significantly reducing costs while enabling large-scale and simultaneous tests worldwide.
