## The Impact of AI on QA Processes

AI is revolutionizing software testing by streamlining workflows, identifying bugs early, and enabling smarter test case generation. Key advancements include:

- **Predictive Analysis**: Using machine learning to highlight high-risk code areas, minimizing development risks.
- **Self-Healing Tests**: AI adapts to changes in UI or code, reducing time spent on test maintenance.
- **Continuous Testing**: Accelerates testing in DevOps cycles, ensuring speed and quality.
