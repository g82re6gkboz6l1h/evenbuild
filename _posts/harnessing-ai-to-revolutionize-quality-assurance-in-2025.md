## Key Challenges Faced by QA Teams

Quality assurance serves as a critical pillar in software development lifecycle management, ensuring functionality, security, and user satisfaction. However, QA testing faces specific hurdles in modern, fast-paced development environments.

### Handling Complex Architectures

Modern applications integrate APIs, global user interfaces, and advanced infrastructures—posing challenges for testing teams aiming for comprehensive coverage.

### Battling Human Error and Repetitious Tasks

Repetitive manual testing increases cognitive fatigue, resulting in missed defects and less effective testing strategies.

### Scaling with Growing Demand

Expanded applications require scalable testing solutions that are difficult to achieve solely through manual efforts.
