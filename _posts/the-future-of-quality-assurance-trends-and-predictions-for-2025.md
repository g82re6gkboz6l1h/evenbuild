## Emerging Technologies Reshaping QA

### 1. Blockchain for Security & Transparency
Blockchain ensures changelogs and testing records are tamper-proof, offering transparent and traceable testing environments that enhance compliance and accountability.

### 2. IoT QA Practices
With IoT expansion in industries like healthcare and automotive, robust QA practices will focus on device compatibility, data security, and stress testing for interconnected environments.

### 3. Quantum Computing Challenges
As quantum computing evolves, QA teams must adapt to complexities involving extreme processing speeds and compatibility challenges with classical systems.

### 4. AI-Powered QA
Intelligent platforms like **Zof AI** automate manual tasks while enhancing issue detection, dynamic test generation, and real-time analytics for improved performance and reliability.
