## 2. Challenges of Traditional QA Methods

Conventional QA approaches involve manual testing and predefined rules. These methods are unable to meet the demands of increasingly complex software applications, which cause bottlenecks in development cycles. Common challenges include:

- **Time-Consuming Processes**: Manual testing is slow and resource-heavy.
- **Human Errors**: Repeated tasks increase the risk of mistakes.
- **Limited Test Coverage**: Complex software ecosystems often go under-tested.
- **Scaling Issues**: Larger projects overwhelm traditional QA strategies.
- **Reactive Testing**: Bugs are identified after deployment rather than being prevented.

AI addresses these challenges by introducing automation, precision, and predictive capabilities that extend beyond traditional approaches.
