Learn about the challenges, innovative technologies changing the QA domain, and effective strategies to prepare your team for the future of testing.

## Challenges QA Teams Face in a Changing Tech Landscape

The technological advancements pushing the industry forward bring their own set of challenges: increased complexity, faster delivery timelines, and growing security vulnerabilities. As agile development and DevOps practices dominate, QA teams must adapt quickly to balance thoroughness and speed while handling interconnected systems and evolving technologies like AI-driven applications. 

QA testers are now tasked with testing dynamic, self-learning AI models and ensuring their functionality and ethical behavior. Such complexities demand innovative tools, methods, and skill sets to meet the increasing demands of software quality through 2025 and beyond.
