AI enables testing to begin earlier (Shift-Left) in the development phase and extend into the production phase (Shift-Right). Continuous feedback loops ensure issues are identified and resolved faster than ever before.

### 3. **Self-Healing Frameworks**  
Self-healing mechanisms within Zof AI detect and fix broken scripts automatically, adapting to application changes and avoiding downtime.

### 4. **Personalization in QA**
