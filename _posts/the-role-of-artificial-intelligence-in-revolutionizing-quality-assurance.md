## Core AI Technologies Reshaping QA Processes

Key AI technologies bring transformative benefits to QA, enabling scalability and accuracy:

### 1. **Machine Learning (ML)**
ML algorithms analyze testing history, detect defect patterns, predict outcomes, and prioritize testing efforts. By learning continuously, ML frameworks optimize QA over time.

### 2. **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**
Through NLP, AI can interpret manual test scripts, comprehend user requirements, and generate executable test cases, reducing human dependence and enhancing clarity.

### 3. **Computer Vision**
For applications dependent on graphical interfaces, computer vision performs automated visual testing, ensuring consistent resolution, alignment, and functionality.

### 4. **Robotic Process Automation (RPA)**
RPA streamlines repetitive tasks like data entry and report generation, while AI integration adds intelligent decision-making during test execution.

### 5. **Predictive Analytics**
By analyzing historical and real-time data, predictive analytics anticipate defects, optimize resource allocation, and heighten system reliability.
