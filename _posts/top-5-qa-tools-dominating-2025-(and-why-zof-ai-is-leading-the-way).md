## Other Must-Know QA Tools for 2025

### Automation Frameworks: The Backbone of QA
Tools like Selenium, Cypress, and Playwright remain indispensable for automating testing processes. Key advances in 2025 include:
- **Cross-Platform Support** for web, mobile, and cloud-based apps.
- **AI Integration** to boost script efficiency.
- **Reusable Test Scripts**, reducing development time.

These frameworks allow teams to handle complex testing environments quickly and effectively.

### Performance Testing: Key to Cloud-Native Success
In an era dominated by cloud and microservices, tools like Apache JMeter and LoadRunner ensure applications perform optimally. Innovations by 2025:
- **Kubernetes and Serverless Integration** for advanced scalability.
- **AI-Driven Bottleneck Detection**, optimizing apps in real time.

### Real-Time Monitoring for QA Teams
Solutions like Dynatrace, Datadog, and New Relic offer unmatched monitoring capabilities, helping QA teams validate in-production performance and identify bugs before users do.

### Collaboration Tools: Current Backbone of DevSecOps
Platforms like Jira, Slack, and GitHub Actions enable unified workflows between QA, DevOps, and engineering teams. Enhanced with AI-driven insights, these tools facilitate better communication and issue resolution.
