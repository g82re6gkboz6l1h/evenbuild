## How AI Shifts the QA Paradigm in 2025

The accelerating digitization and adoption of Agile/DevOps frameworks demand a new approach to QA. Here’s what’s driving the change:

1. **Continuous Delivery Demands**: Agile teams require QA to be faster and integrated within development pipelines, making automation a necessity.
2. **Evolving Technologies**: Applications are becoming smarter, utilizing AI, IoT, and ML technologies, posing new challenges for QA.
3. **High Expectations**: Consumers increasingly expect flawless experiences, especially in industries like healthcare, fintech, and autonomous technologies.

AI amplifies testing by creating smarter workflows and eliminating inefficiencies associated with manual testing. Explore AI-driven benefits like automated test creation, reduced human error, and self-healing capabilities in testing environments.
