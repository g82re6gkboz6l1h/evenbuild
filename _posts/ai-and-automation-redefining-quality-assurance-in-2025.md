Challenges Ahead for QA in 2025

Despite its disruptive potential, adopting AI in QA involves overcoming key challenges:

- **Skill Gaps**: QA professionals need new skills in AI technologies and data analytics.
- **Data Privacy**: AI relies on data-heavy systems, raising security and compliance concerns.
- **Infrastructure Costs**: Initial implementation of AI-driven tools requires financial and technical investment.
- **Change Resistance**: Some teams hesitate to adopt AI due to misconceptions about job replacement or process overhaul.
