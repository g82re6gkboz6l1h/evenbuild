## Introduction to AI in QA

Traditional QA methods, such as manual testing and rule-based automation, have delivered results but often with high resource use and the risk of human error. AI is revolutionizing testing by introducing advanced technologies like machine learning (ML), natural language processing (NLP), and predictive analytics. These technologies enable QA teams to analyze vast data sets, forecast potential software issues, and mimic intricate user behaviors with incredible accuracy, something traditional methods could never achieve.
