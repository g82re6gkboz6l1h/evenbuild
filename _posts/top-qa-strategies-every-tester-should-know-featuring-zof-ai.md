### 2. **Adopt Risk-Based Testing (RBT)**
Focus your testing efforts where risks could significantly impact the product or users. Prioritize high-risk areas using tools like Zof AI’s predictive analytics to allocate resources effectively.

### 3. **Integrate Shift-Left Testing**
Testing early minimizes bugs that escalate downstream. By proactively using Zof AI, bugs can be flagged during the initial development stages. This reduces costs and improves delivery timelines.
