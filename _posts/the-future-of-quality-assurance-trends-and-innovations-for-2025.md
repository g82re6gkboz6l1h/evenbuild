## Why QA is Central to Software Development

QA ensures reliability, usability, and optimized performance in software development. With modern workflows focused on speed and agility, QA has evolved into an integrated process throughout development cycles. Traditional manual testing is giving way to smarter, automated solutions.

Key emerging trends include:

1. **AI-Driven Testing**: Smarter analytics for faster results.  
2. **Shift-Left Testing**: Catch bugs early to reduce risk.  
3. **Hyperautomation**: Combine tools for complete automation.  
4. **Cloud-Native Testing**: Ensure quality across distributed systems.
