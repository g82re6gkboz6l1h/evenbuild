The Rise of Continuous Testing

Continuous testing ensures QA happens concurrently with development:

- **Rapid Feedback**: Developers get immediate insights from automated test scripts.
- **Early Bug Detection**: Fix defects during development stages to save costs and avoid delays.
- **Faster Deployments**: Integrating tools like Zof AI boosts efficiency and reliability.

By embedding QA into the CI/CD pipeline, teams can scale quality assurance processes while delivering consistent high-performance results.
