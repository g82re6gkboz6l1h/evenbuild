## The Top 10 QA Metrics Agile Teams Need

### 1. **Defect Density**
- Tracks defects per 1,000 lines of code (KLOC).
- A **lower defect density** reflects better code quality across sprints.

### 2. **Test Coverage**
- Measures the percentage of code covered by tests.
- Higher coverage reduces undetected bugs, boosting trust in releases.

### 3. **Defect Removal Efficiency (DRE)**
- Indicates defects resolved before production.
- High DRE reduces costly post-release fixes.

### 4. **Leakage Rate**
- Tracks escaped bugs post-launch.
- Lower rates signal robust testing processes.

### 5. **Cycle Time for Bug Fixes**
- Measures time taken to resolve bugs.
- Faster fix times reflect agile responsiveness.

### 6. **Mean Time to Detect (MTTD)**
- Indicates how quickly defects are identified.
- A key measure of testing and monitoring efficiency.

### 7. **Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)**
- Evaluates time between software failures.
- Reveals system performance and stability trends.

### 8. **Automated Test Pass Rate**
- Shows successful automated test completions.
- A way to monitor test reliability and script performance.

### 9. **Escaped Defects**
- Tracks user-reported defects missed in testing.
- Lower rates emphasize strong QA processes.

### 10. **Burndown Charts for QA**
- Visualizes QA task completion during sprints.
- Ensures alignment with team timelines and goals.
