## Overview: Why QA Challenges are Escalating

The role of QA has expanded from bug fixing to ensuring performance, cybersecurity, compliance, and AI-driven solutions across devices. Key factors driving QA complexity include:

1. **Faster Development Timelines:** Agile and DevOps demand faster delivery without quality compromise.
2. **Higher Consumer Expectations:** Users expect flawless performance across platforms.
3. **Complex Ecosystems:** From IoT devices to AI and APIs, QA must ensure robust interconnected systems.
4. **Increased Cybersecurity Risks:** Evolving threats require proactive security measures.

Modern QA professionals must embrace innovative tools and methodologies to address these challenges effectively.
