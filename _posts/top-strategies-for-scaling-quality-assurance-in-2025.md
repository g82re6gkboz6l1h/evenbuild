3. **Massive Data Volumes**: Handling data explosion and real-world simulation is essential.
4. **Skill Gaps**: The need for technically advanced QA professionals to embrace future tech grows.

Best Practices for QA in Accelerated Development
Ensure quality in rapid release cycles using:
1. **Shift-Left Testing**: Minimize early-stage defects.
2. **Test-Driven Development (TDD)**: Prioritize quality through preemptive testing.
3. **Performance Testing Early**: Simulate real-world stress during production phases.
4. **Customer-Centric QA Metrics**: Align QA efforts with user satisfaction data.
5. **Continuous Feedback Loops**: Regularly analyze QA outcomes to improve processes.
