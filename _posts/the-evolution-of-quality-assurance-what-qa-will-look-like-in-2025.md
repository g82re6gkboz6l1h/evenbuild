## The Transformation of QA: Key Trends Leading to 2025

In the past, QA operated in silos, heavily reliant on manual testing methods and slower waterfall systems — often leading to delays and inefficiencies. However, with Agile and DevOps becoming standard methodologies, QA has shifted to an integrated, collaborative, and real-time process.

By 2025, QA will be characterized by:
- Fully automated low-value tasks
- Heightened collaboration between QA teams and developers
- Increased reliance on AI-powered tools

Through smart AI platforms, like Zof AI, QA will evolve into a proactive practice, going beyond bug detection to continuously monitoring quality across development and production.
