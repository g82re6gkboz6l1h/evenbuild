## QA Challenges in 2025

As software scales and becomes more intricate, QA faces hurdles such as:

1. **Scalability Testing**: Ensuring applications perform seamlessly under heavy loads.
2. **Validating AI Models**: Preventing model drift requires advanced monitoring.
3. **Integration Testing**: Achieving interoperability across multi-system environments.
4. **Security Vulnerabilities**: Cybersecurity testing must evolve with emerging threats.
5. **Resource Constraints**: Balancing speed and coverage under stringent deadlines.
