## What AI Brings to QA Processes
Traditional QA methods are often labor-intensive and slow. AI addresses these limitations with automation, predictive analytics, error detection, and test prioritization, reducing manual effort and improving efficiency.

Key contributions of AI to QA:

- **Automated Test Scripts:** AI can generate test scripts for diverse conditions automatically.
- **Predictive Analytics:** Anticipates system bugs and failures.
- **Error Pattern Recognition:** Identifies recurring defects with deep learning.
- **Efficient Test Prioritization:** Determines the most impactful test cases to run first.

AI-driven QA tools are proving invaluable for reducing costs, improving product quality, and accelerating time-to-market.
