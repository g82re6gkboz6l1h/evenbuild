## 2. Resilience Testing: A QA Priority by 2025

In an era of cloud computing and microservices, **resilience testing** ensures applications can withstand unexpected network failures, traffic surges, and cyber threats. By 2025, this will become a mandatory aspect of QA workflows.

### Best Practices for Resilience Testing:
1. **Chaos Engineering:** Simulate unexpected failures to analyze system reliability.
2. **Fault Injection Testing:** Identify weaknesses by introducing deliberate faults into systems.
3. **Disaster Recovery Testing:** Prepare for real-world outages with recovery simulations.

### Benefits:
- Greater user trust through reliable performance.
- Reduced downtime and cost savings.
- Improved scalability for large systems.
