Digital Twins: Simulating Perfection in QA

### What Are Digital Twins?
Digital twins are virtual replicas of physical systems, enabling controlled testing for better performance prediction and system reliability. Applications span industries like healthcare, transportation, and manufacturing.

#### Benefits:
- Cost-effective solutions for real-world testing insights.
- Precise prediction of system performance under various conditions.

By adopting digital twins, QA teams create robust testing ecosystems and reduce inefficiencies.
