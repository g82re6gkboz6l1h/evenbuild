Overcoming AI Challenges in QA

Despite its advantages, AI-driven QA brings challenges:

- **AI Dependency**: Over-reliance may lead to neglecting human insight.
- **Algorithm Bias**: AI tools risk biases in training datasets.
- **Accountability Complexity**: Errors can blur accountability lines.
- **High Initial Costs**: Small businesses worry about scaling expenses.

Solutions like maintaining human oversight, diversifying training data, and scalable pricing models address these concerns effectively.

The Evolving Role of Manual Testing

Hybrid AI-human models promise a balanced approach to QA.
