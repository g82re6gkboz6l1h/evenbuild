## What is AI-Powered Quality Assurance? 

Quality Assurance ensures that software products meet the highest standards before they launch. Historically, QA depended heavily on manual testing and standardized frameworks, which often struggled to keep pace with the rapid development cycles of modern agile and DevOps environments.

AI changes the game by analyzing massive datasets, spotting patterns, and enabling predictive decisions—replicating human cognition at a much faster and more consistent pace.
