## Top QA Best Practices for 2023

1. **Shift Left Testing**: Begin testing early in the development cycle to catch and resolve issues sooner. 
2. **Test Automation**: Use tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) to scale testing and improve efficiency through AI-powered automation.
3. **Cross-Environment Testing**: Ensure consistent performance across various platforms, browsers, and devices.
4. **Exploratory Testing**: Manually uncover edge cases and usability issues beyond scripted tests.
5. **Robust Test Data Strategies**: Utilize synthetic and anonymized data for better testing and compliance with GDPR.

Continuous Testing in CI/CD Pipelines

In 2023, QA must align with Continuous Integration and Delivery (CI/CD). Adopting automated regression testing while incorporating manual validation ensures the quality of every build. Integrating QA into DevOps fosters seamless collaboration and quicker resolutions.

---

Post-Release QA Monitoring

QA doesn't stop at release. Monitor real-world performance to identify issues and collect user feedback. Regular retrospectives based on insights from post-launch data prepare teams for continuous improvement.
