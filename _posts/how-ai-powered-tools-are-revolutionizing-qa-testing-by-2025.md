## AI Evolution in QA Testing
### From Manual Testing to AI Augmentation

1. **Manual Testing**: Time-intensive scripts executed manually by QA engineers.
2. **Automated Testing**: Faster execution with tools like Selenium and Appium but still reliant on significant human input.
3. **AI-Enhanced Testing**: Near-full automation by leveraging data analysis, pattern recognition, and dynamic adaptability.
