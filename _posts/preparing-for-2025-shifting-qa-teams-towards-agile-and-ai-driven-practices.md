## Agile Transformations in QA
Agile methodologies replace slow waterfall workflows with iterative progress, emphasizing continuous feedback and collaboration. QA professionals are now embedded from the planning stages, incorporating testing throughout development.

### Embracing Change
- **Shift-Left Testing:** Early testing reduces defects and streamlines workflows. 
- **Reusable Test Cases:** Scalable testing frameworks foster efficiency.  
- **Rapid Feedback:** Agile sprints demand instantaneous, actionable input.
