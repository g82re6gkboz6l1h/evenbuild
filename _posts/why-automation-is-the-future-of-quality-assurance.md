### Why Automated Testing is Becoming Essential

Modern development demands Agile and DevOps methodologies—rapid release cycles, continuous integration, and constant testing. Traditional manual QA processes struggle to adapt due to:

- **Increasing Complexity:** Software applications now cater to diverse devices, environments, and configurations.
- **Accuracy and Speed Needs:** Manual methods are prone to human error and sluggish workflows.
- **Scalability Limitations:** Testing complex software manually requires extensive time and resources.

Automation addresses these pain points expertly, enabling agile teams to deliver quality faster and efficiently.
