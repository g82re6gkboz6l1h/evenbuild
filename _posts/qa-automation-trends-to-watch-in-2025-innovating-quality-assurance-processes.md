Must-Track Metrics for Superior QA Automation Efficiency

Monitoring your QA automation efforts is crucial. Here are the essential key performance indicators (KPIs) to track in 2025:

1. **Automation Coverage**: Percentage of test cases automated versus total cases.
2. **Execution Speed**: Time taken for test execution relative to deadlines.
3. **Bug Detection Rate**: Effectiveness in identifying bugs during development.
4. **Flakiness Index**: Reliability of test results under varying circumstances.
5. **Script Breakage Resolution Time**: Time taken to detect and fix broken scripts, which can be optimized using tools like Zof AI.
6. **Test ROI**: Balance between the cost of automation tools and benefits like productivity and quality improvements.
