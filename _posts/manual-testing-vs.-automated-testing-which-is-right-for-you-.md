## Key Pros and Cons

### Manual Testing: Where It Shines
**Pros**:
1. Human-driven creativity uncovers unique errors.
2. Superior for assessing user experience and UI.
3. Adaptive and ideal for ad hoc scenarios.

**Cons**:
1. Time-intensive for large systems.
2. Prone to human errors.
3. Inefficient for repetitive tasks.

### Automated Testing: The Efficiency Expert
**Pros**:
1. Quicker execution and scalable for DevOps pipelines.
2. Ideal for regression checks and repetitive workflows.
3. Enables thorough testing of system loads and performance.

**Cons**:
1. Requires initial investment in setup and maintenance.
2. Limited usability testing capabilities.
3. Difficulty handling dynamic or complex workflows.
