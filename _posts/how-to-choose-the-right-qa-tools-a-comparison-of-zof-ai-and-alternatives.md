## What to Consider When Selecting QA Tools
### 1. **Ease of Use**
Efficient testing requires tools with user-friendly interfaces and minimal onboarding time.
### 2. **Feature Set**
Must-haves include automation, CI/CD integration, comprehensive cross-device coverage, and actionable reporting.
### 3. **Scalability**
The tool should expand with your growing infrastructure and evolving app requirements.
### 4. **Cost-Effectiveness**
Evaluate pricing models that align with your team size and future scaling.
### 5. **Support Infrastructure**
A solid support network reduces downtime when issues arise.
