## Why QA Matters More Than Ever

### Key Challenges for QA Teams in 2025:
1. **Complex Systems:** IoT devices, microservices, and cloud architectures introduce unprecedented intricacies.
2. **User Expectations:** Applications must be flawless and intuitive—failure risks significant reputation damage.
3. **Cybersecurity:** Advanced cyberattacks demand rigorous vulnerability testing and penetration checks.
4. **Faster Development Timelines:** QA teams must adapt to shorter lifecycles without compromising quality.
