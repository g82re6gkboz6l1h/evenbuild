## Current Trends in QA Testing

QA testing is adapting to new methodologies like DevOps, agile workflows, and continuous delivery. Here are the key trends shaping the field:

### 1. Shift to Automation
Automation tools like Selenium, Cypress, and Playwright dominate QA processes, enhancing scalability and efficiency.

### 2. Shift-Left Approach
Testing is now integrated earlier in development cycles, reducing costs and improving delivery speed.

### 3. Continuous Testing
CI/CD pipelines incorporating tools like Jenkins ensure functionality testing at every stage.

### 4. Enterprise-Scale QA
Cloud-based testing and containerized environments such as Docker enable streamlined testing across platforms.
