## What is AI-Powered Quality Assurance?

AI-powered quality assurance integrates intelligent algorithms into the testing process, eliminating human mistakes and enhancing efficiency. While manual testing is prone to errors and automated scripts demand upkeep, AI adapts dynamically.

### Key Benefits of AI in QA:
1. **Adaptive Automation:** AI adjusts to software changes, unlike static traditional automation tools.
2. **Machine Learning-Driven Testing:** AI systems learn from patterns to continuously optimize processes.
3. **Defect Prediction:** Pinpoints critical areas prone to issues for focused testing.
4. **Behavior Simulation:** Mirrors user actions to uncover usability flaws.
