## What is Hyper-Automation in QA?

Hyper-automation in QA represents a fusion of AI, ML, RPA, and intelligent bots, enabling QA teams to automate end-to-end processes in software development. This next-gen approach addresses complexities in software testing, including mobile apps, cross-platform environments, APIs, and microservices.

Key aspects of hyper-automation in QA include:

- **End-to-End Automation:** Streamlining testing across the software lifecycle.
- **Smart Insights:** AI-based analytics to predict and mitigate potential issues.
- **Self-Healing Scripts:** Autonomous systems dynamically adapting as applications evolve.
- **Cross-Platform Scalability:** Automated testing that ensures performance across diverse platforms.
