## What is AI-Assisted Testing?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming software development, particularly in the realm of Quality Assurance (QA). AI-assisted testing leverages machine learning and intelligent algorithms to streamline, enhance, and automate testing processes in software development lifecycles. Compared to traditional manual-heavy testing, AI-powered tools rely on predictive analytics, pattern recognition, and automation to deliver faster, more reliable results.

### Benefits of AI-Assisted Testing:
- Automated testing for repetitive tasks.
- Faster bug detection and resolution.
- Data-driven insights for software performance.
- Continuous learning for accuracy and efficiency.

In today's agile development environment, AI in QA solves challenges like speed, scalability, and reliability, propelling businesses toward high-quality software releases.
