## Overview of Major QA Challenges

QA testing ensures smooth system functionality, but hurdles arise:

- **Tight Deadlines**: Accelerated schedules lead to insufficient testing.
- **Resource Gaps**: Teams may lack adequate personnel and funding.
- **Software Complexity**: Modern systems demand intricate validations.
- **Poor Test Scenarios**: Limited or unrealistic test cases compromise accuracy.
- **Scalability Issues**: Agile workflows challenge traditional approaches.
