Potential Challenges in AI QA Adoption
Despite the breakthroughs, incorporating AI tools presents hurdles. Consider these factors as organizations migrate to advanced systems:

1. **Steep Learning Curve**: Staff requires extensive training to proficiently operate AI tools.
2. **Data Quality Dependency**: Faulty training datasets could lead to inaccurate results and undetected bugs.
3. **Initial Investment**: AI tools such as Zof AI demand significant upfront costs, making ROI a medium-term objective.
4. **Legacy System Compatibility**: Integration barriers with older infrastructure may slow implementation.
5. **Ethical Concerns**: AI systems need bias-free datasets to ensure inclusive testing practices.
