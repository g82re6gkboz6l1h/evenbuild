## How AI Redefines QA Processes
AI's ability to analyze large datasets, detect patterns, and flag anomalies will dramatically enhance QA processes in the coming years. Key transformations include:
  
- **Automated Defect Detection**: AI systems will accurately and efficiently identify code errors.
- **Dynamic Testing**: Endless simulation scenarios in less time.
- **Self-learning Capabilities**: Machine learning algorithms will adapt testing strategies based on application behavior.

Traditional manual testing will shift to predictive and automated approaches, improving both speed and accuracy for QA professionals.
