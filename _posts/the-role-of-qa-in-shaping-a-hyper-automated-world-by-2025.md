## What is Hyper-Automation?

Hyper-automation integrates AI, ML, robotic processes, and advanced algorithms to fully automate complex operations across industries like healthcare, finance, retail, and manufacturing. Unlike traditional automation, hyper-automation scales end-to-end processes, enhancing operational efficiency and customer experiences at unprecedented speed.

### Why QA is Critical to Hyper-Automation’s Success
- **Ensures Accuracy:** QA validates algorithms and workflows, especially for high-stakes sectors like finance or healthcare.
- **Scalability Focus:** QA verifies systems handle exponential data growth while maintaining performance stability.
- **Compliance Safeguards:** Industries under regulations ensure QA maintains traceability and adheres to standards like GDPR.
- **Continuous Improvement:** Iterative QA evolves systems for better bug rectification and feature enhancements.

Hyper-automation relies on robust QA protocols to deliver seamless functionality across interconnected systems.

2. **Continuous Monitoring:** Real-time insights optimize system reliability.
