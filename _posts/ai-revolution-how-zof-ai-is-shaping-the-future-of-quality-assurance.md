4. **Smarter Defect Analysis:** AI pinpoints issues faster, providing actionable insights.  
5. **Predictive Analytics:** Tools like Zof AI forecast problem areas for proactive testing.  
6. **Real-World User Simulations:** AI enhances user experience testing and ensures alignment with end-user expectations.
