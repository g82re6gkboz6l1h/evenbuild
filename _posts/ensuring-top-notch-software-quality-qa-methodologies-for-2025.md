## Transforming QA Methodologies for Tomorrow

From Agile to DevOps, QA has evolved into a continuous process deeply embedded in development workflows. By 2025, QA strategies will integrate:

- **Shift-Left Testing**: Tackling bugs earlier with preemptive testing during development.
- **Continuous QA**: Making QA an integral, automated part of CI/CD pipelines.
- **Observability in Testing**: Monitoring production systems in real-time to capture and resolve issues efficiently.
