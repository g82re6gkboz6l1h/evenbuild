## AI's Role in Modern Quality Assurance

AI has significantly impacted QA in software development, driving automation, predicting risks, and enhancing overall efficiency. The technology empowers developers with tools capable of: 

- **Adaptive Test Automation**: Eliminating manual workflows by dynamically adjusting test coverage per code changes. 
- **Defect Prediction**: Pinpointing vulnerabilities based on data-driven insights. 
- **Optimized Regression Testing**: Automatically identifying testing priorities after application updates.
- **Performance Monitoring**: Recognizing and resolving bottlenecks through predictive analytics.

AI-enabled platforms utilize advanced machine learning models and natural language processing (NLP) functionalities to revolutionize QA operations—building smarter, faster workflows.
