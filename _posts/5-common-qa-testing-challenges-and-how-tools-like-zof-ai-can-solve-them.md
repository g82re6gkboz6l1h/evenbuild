## 1. The Key QA Pain Points You Need to Overcome

The purpose of QA testing is to validate that a product aligns with its design requirements, meets user needs, and delivers error-free performance. Despite being necessary, QA teams often struggle with common roadblocks:

1. **Scalability Problems**: Larger software systems demand testing at scale. Manual methods and traditional tools fall short in keeping up.
2. **Outdated Test Processes**: Adapting old test scripts to new updates takes time, impacting overall efficiency.
3. **Excessive QA Costs**: QA operations often strain both financial and human resources, particularly when scaling efforts.
4. **Integration Challenges**: Integrating QA within CI/CD systems and ensuring compatibility with third-party frameworks can significantly delay processes.
