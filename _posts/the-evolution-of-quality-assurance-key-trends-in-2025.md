## The Rise of Intelligent QA Practices by 2025

In early development stages, QA focused on manual processes and functionality testing. As systems became more complex, QA evolved into a sophisticated, integrated practice serving scalability and user experience needs. By 2025, QA features AI-powered automation, predictive analytics, and continuous deployment alignment, making it an indispensable part of the software lifecycle.
