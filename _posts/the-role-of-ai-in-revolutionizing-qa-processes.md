### 1. **Accelerated Testing**

Modern systems demand an ability to test vast datasets and complex integrations efficiently. AI ensures scalability, whether managing consumer-facing applications or executing backend infrastructure tests.

Human testers often face challenges providing exhaustive coverage. AI-driven tools offer enhanced test coverage, eliminating potential bugs and improving product reliability.
