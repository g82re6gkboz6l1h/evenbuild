## How AI is Revolutionizing QA Processes
AI enhances both manual and automated testing by delivering scalable solutions that adapt, learn, and execute autonomously. Traditional QA processes rely on human input for tasks like creating test cases. AI eliminates inefficiencies with capabilities such as:

- **Automated Test Creation**: AI generates test scripts from user behavior data, saving time and minimizing human errors.
- **Bug Predictions**: Machine learning models analyze applications to detect likely defect-prone areas.
- **Self-Healing Scripts**: AI dynamically updates test scripts in response to frequent UI changes, reducing maintenance.
- **Visual Regression Testing**: AI systems identify UI anomalies across app versions with exceptional accuracy.

As AI becomes integral to QA, businesses must prioritize ethical concerns including:

- **Transparency**: Providing QA teams insight into AI-driven decisions.
