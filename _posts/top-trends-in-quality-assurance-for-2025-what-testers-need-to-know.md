## Generative AI: Revolutionizing QA Processes

Generative AI represents a significant leap in QA practices, automating test case creation, system failure prediction, and writing complex code snippets for testing. It improves coverage of edge cases and boosts efficiency dramatically. By 2025, this technology will be a standard across QA processes, requiring testers to build expertise in AI-driven platforms.
