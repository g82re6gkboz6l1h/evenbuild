As software systems grow increasingly complex, 2025 marks a pivotal moment where next-generation tools and strategies will redefine QA practices, ensuring seamless application performance and reliability. This article dives into key innovations driving QA forward, including AI advancements, automated workflows, and the integration of IoT and cloud-based insights.
