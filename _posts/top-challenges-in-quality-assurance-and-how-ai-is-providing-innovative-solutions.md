## Common QA Challenges That Teams Face

1. **Limited Test Coverage:** Under tight deadlines, achieving thorough test coverage becomes almost impossible.
2. **Human Errors in Manual Testing:** Inconsistent reporting and unnoticed defects can compromise software quality.
3. **Time Constraints:** The rapid pace of Agile and DevOps methodologies leaves little room for extensive testing.
4. **Resource Limitations:** Small teams have limited capacity for handling complex scenarios.
5. **Scaling Difficulties:** QA processes must scale alongside growing applications, which is inherently challenging.
6. **Testing Bottlenecks:** Dependencies between workflows cause major delays.

These challenges hinder QA teams from meeting the dual demands of speed and quality, necessitating innovative solutions.

## The Limitations of Traditional QA Methods

### Manual Testing
Manual testing allows for subjective problem-solving but is time-consuming, prone to errors, and difficult to scale effectively.

### Automated Testing
Automation excels in speed and precision, but it requires significant upfront investment and lacks adaptability for complex, dynamic environments. Despite its advantages, automation alone cannot address scalability, test coverage, and human oversight limitations.
